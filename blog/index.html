Welcome to my blog!

I was once an avid public blogger. I owned a blog at Google's Blogspot and then had a brief stint at Wordpress. As I became more geeky with each passing day, I realized I did not feel like using those tools. Slowly, I used Flask and a few other nifty tools to transfer all my blog posts to Github pages. I've divided my blog into two major sections - a General section and a Geek section. My thoughts on general aspects of life, culture etc are present in the general section while my attempts to share some geeky stuff is / will be captured in the latter section. Hope you enjoy your sojourn here.
