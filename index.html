Welcome to my home page.

Hello! My name is Kaustubh Vaghmare. I am currently working as a Data Scientist at the Inter-University Centre for Astronomy and Astrophysics (IUCAA). My interests lie in understanding the challenges in the maintenance, storage, analysis and sharing of astronomical data and to study best practices to handle them.

I have only recently taken up this position in IUCAA. Before this, I was a PhD student at IUCAA and very recently defended my thesis titled Structure, Properties and Formation Histories of S0 Galaxies. As can be guessed from the thesis title, I am interested in observational studies of galaxies i.e. using their images and spectra to unravel their origins and inter-relationships with the cosmos.

I have also worked / am working in other areas of Astronomy - counterpart identifcation of X-ray sources, variability estimation of astrophysical sources and Astrostatistics. I am an avid user of the Python programming language and have spent a good deal of time encouraging the adoption of the language among my colleagues.
