Welcome to my home page!

Who Am I?

I am a PhD in Astrophysics from IUCAA currently working as a Data Scientist for a national level project titled Data Driven Initiatives in Astronomy and Biology.

What do I do?

As a data scientist, I handle lot of things that are data related. I manage projects related to data curation, archival, dissemination and analysis/mining. I help leverage Machine Learning / Deep Learning for astronomical problems. I design web applications to enable astronomers access data. And I consult on choice of technologies, both software and hardware to solve data related problenms.

What can you find here?

A lot! From a description of PhD work to my current work, along with resources used by me during teaching & training as well as my thoughts on things, both general and geeky! Hope you have a nice time here.
