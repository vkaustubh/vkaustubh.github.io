And When I am Not…

I have always been fond of reading. Starting from Enid Blyton’s novels, moving slowly towards Nancy Drew and Hardy Boys I have eventually climbed the ladder to read Mario Puzo, Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, Danielle Steele, Sidney Sheldon, Ayn Rand, Isaac Asimov and others. I have no specific inclination to read only a specific type of book. I am open both to fiction and non fiction. In non-fiction, I am particularly fascinated by the great stalwarts of science popularization such as Carl Sagan and Richard Dawkins. I usually make an intuitive guess as to whether I want to read something or not.

I love watching movies. I am open to watching several kinds of movies. Anything that inspires, alerts, moves, educates and entertains! Some of my favorite actors include Al Pacino and Tom Hanks. There are several other favorites but these two stand out the most.

Extremely fond of music. My interest ranges from soft tunes of classical all the way to heavy metal. Classical would include both western and eastern. Currently trying to dwell into a lot of Mozart. Genres include progressive rock, progressive metal, trash metal, speed metal, psychedelic rock, Western classical, Jazz and a few more. I am also trying to free my fingers on the guitar.

In sports and games, I love Carroms. I also play Badminton now and then. I like playing Chess but has been a while. As far as watching is concerned, I hardly do that! But if given a chance, I love watching Snooker, Pool, Boxing and Tennis.
